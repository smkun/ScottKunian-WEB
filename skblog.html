Python: My Silver Lining After Layoff

Meet the 53-year-old IT pro...

Life took an unexpected turn when I found myself amongst the laid-off employees. The company I dedicated years to decided to downsize, leaving me in a state of shock. But you know what they say—when one door closes, another opens. So, I dusted off my coding skills and embarked on a new adventure: learning Python.

"Python’s simplicity and versatility fascinated me. From automating mundane tasks to diving into data science, Python has become my beacon of hope. Every day with a cup tea by my side (thanks Lori), I dive into Python tutorials. I imagine creating my own applications, solving real-world problems, and maybe even contributing to open-source projects. Age is just a number when passion fuels the journey.""

Rolling the Dice: Tabletop Adventures

Gathering Around the Table...

My love for tabletop gaming transcends pixels and screens. Every week, I invite my friends over for epic adventures. The 3rd floor gaming table transforms into a battleground, and dice become the arbiters of fate. Whether it’s Dungeons & Dragons, Magic the Gathering or some other RPG, we revel in the camaraderie and creativity.

As a dungeon master, I weave intricate narratives. My friends embody warriors, wizards, and rogues, their laughter filling the room. We strategize, negotiate, and sometimes fail spectacularly. But it’s not about winning; it’s about escaping reality, sharing laughter, and creating memories. My favorite moments? When a critical roll determines the fate of the realm, and everyone leans in, breath held.

We miss you every single game Tony.

Furry Companions: Zula, Peanut, and Daphney

Meet the Fur Family...

My home isn’t complete without my furry companions. Zula, the majestic Rottweiler, guards the house with unwavering loyalty. Her eyes hold secrets of countless squirrel chases and moonlit patrols. Peanut, the fat white cat, perches on windowsills, observing the world with curiosity. Daphney, the elegant Russian blue, demands attention with her regal meows.

After a tough day, their presence brings solace. Zula's tail thumps against the floor, welcoming me home. Peanut curls up on my lap, purring contentment. Daphney weaves figure eights around my legs, reminding me that life's simple pleasures matter(or she's trying to trip me). They don't care about layoffs or Python syntax—they offer love, warmth, and a reminder that sometimes, the best companions have fur and whiskers.
